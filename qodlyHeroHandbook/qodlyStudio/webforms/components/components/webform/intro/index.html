Powerful Webform Building: Components and Tools Explored


As I strode purposefully towards Qodly Studio, I knew that I had the power to create a web masterpiece that would leave our users spellbound.


The Webform Component: The Base Container​


Similar to how QDroid is the guiding force, the Webform component serves as the cornerstone, encapsulating all other elements. It empowers me to fashion a visually striking and interactive homepage.

With unwavering determination, I commanded QDroid, proclaiming:

🗣️  QDroid, let's establish the foundation of our website by transforming the Webform component's background image into a captivating representation of myself, the esteemed leader of the heroic Qodly team.


The Properties Panel: A Comprehensive Tool​


With QDroid's invaluable assistance, we swiftly accessed the Properties panel of the Webform component and seamlessly modified the background image to a captivating depiction of myself. Setting the background image size to Cover, we witnessed the entire container being enveloped in the magnificence of my presence.

The Properties panel is truly remarkable, offering an extensive range of options to modify various aspects of a component. If you wish to unlock the full potential of this powerful tool, make sure to delve into the comprehensive Properties panel documentation for an enriching exploration.

See Properties Panel documentation for more information.

The Impressive Arsenal of Tools​


As we delve deeper into the Webform component, it's crucial to check the results of each modification live. But before we do that, let me showcase the three impressive arsenal of tools available for managing your webform components.


①   Tooltip & Breadcrumbs​

The first tool we'll talk about as we embark on this journey of exploring webform components is the Tooltip. It's a smart and intuitive feature that offers a range of actions to choose from.

See Tooltip documentation for more information.

Moving on, we have the Breadcrumbs feature located right above. This feature displays the hierarchy of your component selection. As you navigate through the trail, you can click on any parent component name to select it. This allows for easy management of your components and streamlines your workflow.


②   Header Panel​

Next on our list of features is the Header panel. This comprehensive tool offers various functionalities, such as a quick scan for issues to fix, background color identification of bound datasources, real-time preview of webform changes, and the ability to render the webform in a new tab.


See Header Panel documentation for more information.

③   Contextual Panel​

Last but not least, the Contextual Panel empowers you to bind webform events to different actions, such as executing functions, navigating to other webforms, or adding standard actions. It provides a customizable and dynamic experience to meet your unique requirements.


See Contextual Panel documentation for more information.


After setting the visual foundation of our homepage, it is time to focus on crafting a brief yet compelling description of our website's purpose. Let's explore the next section and learn how we tackled this crucial aspect of web design. Additionally, we will have the opportunity to check the live results of the modifications we made through the Preview button , ensuring a seamless and captivating user experience.
